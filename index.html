Privacy Policy for MoodSnap

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MoodSnap collects photos taken through the app's camera feature solely for mood detection functionality. All photos and mood data are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers.

2. Camera Permission

MoodSnap requires camera access to capture selfies for mood analysis. The camera permission is used exclusively for this purpose. Photos are processed locally using AI technology and stored on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

The photos you take are analyzed using artificial intelligence to detect facial expressions and determine your emotional state. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not access, collect, or transmit your photos or mood data to any external servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage

All mood entries and photos are stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This data remains private and under your control at all times.

5. Third-Party Services

MoodSnap uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect certain information such as device identifiers and usage data for advertising purposes. This information is collected and processed by Google according to their privacy policy. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information or photos to any third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes, as described above.

7. Data Security

Your photos and mood data are stored securely on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data, including photos and mood entries, at any time by uninstalling the MoodSnap application from your device. This will permanently remove all locally stored information.

9. Children's Privacy

MoodSnap is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
